# 👥 HR Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
Developed a Power BI dashboard to help HR teams analyze employee 
attrition, performance, and workforce demographics.

## Problem Statement
The HR department had no visibility into why employees were 
leaving. This dashboard identifies key attrition drivers and 
helps in better workforce planning.

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- Microsoft Excel (data preprocessing)
- DAX (measures for attrition rate, avg tenure)

## Key Features
- Employee attrition rate by department and age group
- Salary vs attrition analysis
- Job satisfaction and performance rating breakdown
- Gender and education-wise workforce distribution
- KPI cards: Total Employees, Attrition Rate, Avg Age, Avg Salary

## Key Insights
- Employees with less than 2 years tenure had highest attrition
- Sales department showed 25% higher attrition than average
- Low salary band employees were 3x more likely to leave
